Health Ninjas

Projects Description

  • Independently developed a 3D interactive game to integrate health education into biofeedback treatment for childhood obesity. Adopting reward and penalty scheme to explore potential HCI benefits.
  • We propose an effective and developmentally-appropriate biofeedback game that teaches children deep breathing and nutrition knowledge.
  • Designed algorithms to dynamically adapt the game scenario based on physiologoical data Designed experimental protocols and conducted user studies at local children community.
